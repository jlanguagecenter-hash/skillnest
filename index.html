About SkillNest

SkillNest started with a simple observation: many talented students in Bangladesh want to study or work in Japan, but struggle to find Japanese language training that is both structured and taught in a language they're comfortable in. We built our program to close exactly that gap.

Our classes follow Minna no Nihongo, the textbook most widely used in Japanese language schools, so what you learn here lines up with what you'll be expected to know once you arrive in Japan. Every lesson is explained in Bangla first, then reinforced in Japanese — grammar, vocabulary, and JLPT-style practice questions included.

  • Experienced, bilingual teachers with small batch sizes
  • JLPT-style mock tests every month, with detailed feedback
  • Dedicated embassy & school interview coaching before departure
  • All classes conducted live online — no commute, no missed lessons

A Note From Our Lead Instructor

Every year I meet students who are capable of far more than they believe — they just need the right structure and someone explaining things in a language that actually makes sense to them. That's what we try to be at SkillNest: not just a language course, but a steady hand from your first Japanese word to your interview day.
